About Smithy

Formerly known as Team Jimin


Welcome to Smithy, the devo-network building website. Where you can reach for your stars and land back on Earth.
This was a website built by Team Jimin, consisting of Alejandro, Noakai, Justin, and Ivan.

This website was created as our final project for our Software Development Class under the guidance of Mr.Mykolyk. It is meant to help facilitate the creation of projects and give oppurtunities to connect with your fellow classmates in a brand new clean website! The site will allow teams to upload their projects to the server, rate and comment on these projects. You can upvote and downvote comments as well as a star system to rate the overall projects of your peers. Make your way towards a Devo-Emertius.
